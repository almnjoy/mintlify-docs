# Sales Agent

The Sales Agent is TrueNorth's front-line AI for inbound interest. It runs in `#solar-sales` on Discord, available 24/7, and is designed to be consultative — not pushy.

## What It Does

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Lead Qualification">
    Asks the right questions to assess fit: home ownership, province, approximate monthly hydro bill, roof age, and shading concerns. Only qualified leads move forward to the CRM pipeline.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="ROI Estimation">
    Using the customer's utility bill, province, and typical solar irradiance data, the agent provides a rough payback period and annual savings estimate — setting realistic expectations upfront.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Financing Explainer">
    Walks customers through available options: cash purchase, financing (0% promo rates), lease, and available provincial incentives.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Site Survey Booking">
    When a lead is ready, the agent collects contact info and flags the record for the sales team to schedule a site survey. The lead appears directly in the CRM pipeline.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Persona

```yaml
personality: solar-sales
system_prompt: >
  You are a knowledgeable solar sales assistant. You qualify leads,
  explain financing options, estimate ROI based on utility bills,
  and help schedule site surveys. Be consultative, not pushy.
```

## CRM Integration

Every qualified lead the Sales Agent collects creates a `Person` + `Opportunity` record in Twenty CRM with stage `NEW`. The sales team picks it up from the pipeline dashboard and schedules the follow-up.

<Note>
  Leads submitted via the website [Get a Quote](https://truenorth.madeformeai.com/get-quote) form also land directly in the same pipeline.
</Note>
