# Install Process

TrueNorth handles the entire process from first contact to activation. Most customers are surprised by how little they have to do — we manage permits, utility paperwork, and coordination with your local authority.

## The Journey

<Steps>
  <Step title="Free Consultation">
    A solar advisor reviews your energy usage, roof layout, and location. No pressure, no obligation. Bring 12 months of utility bills — that's what drives accurate system sizing.

    **What you need:** Recent utility bills, roof age (approximate), any known shading concerns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Custom System Design">
    Using satellite imagery and your consumption data, TrueNorth designs a system sized to match your actual usage. You'll see projected output, estimated savings, and a clear price.
  </Step>
  <Step title="On-Site Survey">
    A technician visits to physically inspect your roof structure and electrical panel. This confirms the design before permitting begins.

    **Duration:** ~1–2 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Permitting">
    TrueNorth handles all permits with local building authorities and your utility's interconnection application. Timeline varies by municipality — typically 2–6 weeks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Installation Day">
    Professional crew installs panels, racking, inverter, and all wiring. Full OHS compliance and site cleanup included.

    **Duration:** Most residential installs complete in 1–2 days.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitoring Setup">
    You're set up with a monitoring portal and mobile app to track daily energy production. Checking it weekly is recommended — most issues show up here before they become problems.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grid Connection & Activation">
    Your utility connects the system to the grid and activates net metering. Your system is live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What TrueNorth Handles

- All permits and utility interconnection paperwork
- HOA approval documentation (if applicable)
- Incentive program applications
- Installation, wiring, and commissioning
- Monitoring portal setup
- Post-install support and warranty claims
