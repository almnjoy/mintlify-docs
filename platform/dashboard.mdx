# Dashboard

The TrueNorth team dashboard is available at [truenorth.madeformeai.com](https://truenorth.madeformeai.com/login). It's protected by Authentik SSO — you'll be redirected to sign in with your team account.

## CRM Integration

The dashboard connects live to **Twenty CRM** via GraphQL API. Any lead created through:
- The website quote form
- A Discord Sales Agent conversation

...appears automatically in the pipeline as a `NEW` stage opportunity.

## Access Levels

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| **User** | Dashboard, Pipeline, Team, Resources, Account |
| **Admin/Manager** | All of the above + Agent Brain (AI infrastructure) |

## Signing In

1. Go to [truenorth.madeformeai.com/login](https://truenorth.madeformeai.com/login)
2. Click **Sign In**
3. You'll be redirected to the TrueNorth Authentik login page
4. Sign in with your team credentials
5. You'll be redirected back to the dashboard

<Note>
  First-time access? Contact your account manager to get your credentials set up in Authentik.
</Note>
